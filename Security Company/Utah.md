# Utah Security Company License

## 1. License Name

- Contract Security Company License
- Regulated by Utah Division of Professional Licensing

## 2. Core Requirement

- Utah requires a contract security company license for a business providing contract security services.

## 3. Owner / Qualification Requirements

- Utah uses a company license and a company qualifier structure
- Replacement company qualifier is a dedicated state license action

## 4. Business Requirements

- Apply through Utah DOPL
- Use the exact contract security company application

## 5. Insurance / Bond

- Confirm current Utah insurance and bond filing requirements in the DOPL application materials before filing

## 6. Fees & Validity

- Utah uses DOPL license fees for the company and related individual credentials
- Confirm the current exact company fee in the Utah system

## 7. Employee Guard Requirements

- Unarmed and armed private security officers are licensed separately

## 8. Simple Summary

1. Form the company
2. Set the Utah company qualifier
3. Apply through DOPL for the contract security company license
4. Confirm current fee and insurance items in the Utah application
5. License unarmed and armed officers separately
