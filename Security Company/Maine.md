# Maine Security Company License

## 1. License Name

- Contract Security Company License
- Regulated by Maine State Police

## 2. Core Requirement

- Maine licenses the company rather than requiring each employee to hold a full separate security-company license.

## 3. Owner / Qualification Requirements

- Must be at least 18
- Must be a U.S. citizen or resident alien
- Good moral character required
- No disqualifying recent or serious criminal history under Maine law

## 4. Business Requirements

- Apply for the contract security company license through Maine State Police / DPS

## 5. Insurance / Bond

- Confirm the current insurance or bond filing requirements with Maine State Police before submitting the application

## 6. Fees & Validity

- Confirm the current company fee directly with Maine’s current licensing materials

## 7. Employee Guard Requirements

- Employees of a licensed contract security company are generally covered through the company-license structure
- Armed or special-duty requirements should still be checked before deployment

## 8. Simple Summary

1. Form the company
2. Make sure the owner meets Maine’s character and background standards
3. File for the contract security company license
4. Confirm current fee and insurance items with Maine State Police
