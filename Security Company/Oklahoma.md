# Oklahoma Security Company License

## 1. License Name

- Security Agency License through CLEET
- Regulated by Oklahoma Council on Law Enforcement Education and Training

## 2. Core Requirement

- Oklahoma uses a state licensing structure for private security agencies and individual guards.

## 3. Owner / Qualification Requirements

- Oklahoma requires the company and individuals to meet CLEET qualifications
- Guard and investigator exams are part of the CLEET system

## 4. Business Requirements

- Apply through CLEET for the company license
- Use the Oklahoma private security forms and testing structure

## 5. Insurance / Bond

- Confirm current bond or insurance requirements in the current CLEET packet before filing

## 6. Fees & Validity

- Oklahoma uses CLEET application and licensing fees
- Confirm the exact company fee directly with CLEET when filing

## 7. Employee Guard Requirements

- Guards must complete CLEET-approved training and testing
- CLEET issues the individual licenses

## 8. Simple Summary

1. Form the company
2. Use the CLEET company licensing process
3. Confirm current fee and insurance items with CLEET
4. Train and test each guard through the CLEET system
