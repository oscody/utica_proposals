# Indiana Security Company License

## 1. License Name

- Security Guard Agency License
- Regulated by the Indiana Professional Licensing Agency

## 2. Core Requirement

- Indiana requires a state security guard agency license.
- The applicant must meet the experience or education standard.

## 3. Owner / Qualification Requirements

- Must be at least 21
- Must have either:
  - at least 4,000 hours of investigative and/or security-guard-field experience, or
  - a bachelor’s degree or higher in criminal justice or a related field

## 4. Business Requirements

- Apply through MyLicense One
- Nonresident companies must register as foreign corporations if applicable

## 5. Insurance / Bond

- Evidence of financial responsibility required
- Minimum general liability coverage: $100,000

## 6. Fees & Validity

- Indiana uses board-set fees
- Confirm the current exact application and renewal fees in PLA’s online system

## 7. Employee Guard Requirements

- Indiana separately regulates employee registration and firearms licensing tied to agency work

## 8. Simple Summary

1. Form the company
2. Make sure the applicant meets the experience or degree rule
3. Obtain the required insurance
4. Apply through Indiana PLA
5. Handle employee registration and firearms rules as needed
