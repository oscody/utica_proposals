# New Mexico Security Company License

## 1. License Name

- Security Company License
- Regulated by New Mexico Regulation and Licensing Department

## 2. Core Requirement

- New Mexico requires a state company license for a private security business.

## 3. Owner / Qualification Requirements

- New Mexico licenses the company and separately licenses qualifying managers and employees
- Background review applies

## 4. Business Requirements

- Apply through the RLD private investigations and security program

## 5. Insurance / Bond

- Confirm current bond or insurance requirements in the current New Mexico application materials before filing

## 6. Fees & Validity

- New Mexico uses agency fees through the state licensing program
- Confirm the current exact amount with RLD when filing

## 7. Employee Guard Requirements

- Security guards are licensed separately in New Mexico

## 8. Simple Summary

1. Form the company
2. Apply for the New Mexico company license
3. Confirm current bond and fee items with RLD
4. License employees separately
