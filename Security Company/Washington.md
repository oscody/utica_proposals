# Washington Security Company License

## 1. License Name

- Private Security Company License
- Regulated by Washington Department of Licensing

## 2. Core Requirement

- Washington requires a state private security company license.

## 3. Owner / Qualification Requirements

- The company must meet Washington licensing requirements and maintain a designated trainer / qualifying structure as required by state law

## 4. Business Requirements

- Apply through Washington Department of Licensing
- Company and employee credentials are both part of the Washington system

## 5. Insurance / Bond

- Confirm the current bond or insurance requirement in the Washington application materials before filing

## 6. Fees & Validity

- Washington uses DOL state fees for company and employee licensing
- Confirm the current exact company fee directly with DOL when filing

## 7. Employee Guard Requirements

- Washington private security guards are licensed separately
- Armed guards have added firearms requirements

## 8. Simple Summary

1. Form the company
2. Apply for the Washington private security company license
3. Confirm current fee and insurance items with DOL
4. License employees separately
