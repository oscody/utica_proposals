# New York Security Company License

## 1. License Name

- Watch, Guard or Patrol Agency License
- Regulated by the New York Department of State, Division of Licensing Services

## 2. Core Requirement

- New York requires a state watch, guard, or patrol agency license to operate a security company.
- This file is statewide only and does not cover NYC-specific operating rules.

## 3. Owner / Qualification Requirements

- Applicant must show the experience or background required by New York law
- New York is one of the stricter states for agency qualification and background review

## 4. Business Requirements

- Apply through New York Division of Licensing Services
- Branch offices and business details must match the state licensing records

## 5. Insurance / Bond

- $10,000 surety bond required

## 6. Fees & Validity

- New York uses agency license and renewal fees through DOS
- Confirm the current exact fee directly with the official NY application materials when filing

## 7. Employee Guard Requirements

- Guards must be separately registered as New York security guards
- Armed guards require additional firearms and training compliance

## 8. Simple Summary

1. Form the company
2. Make sure the applicant qualifies under New York’s experience rules
3. Post the $10,000 surety bond
4. Apply for the Watch, Guard or Patrol Agency license
5. Register all guards separately
