# Kentucky Security Company License

## 1. License Name

- No separate statewide private security company license found

## 2. Core Requirement

- Kentucky does not appear to require a statewide security company license.
- Some local jurisdictions may license armed security agencies or guards.

## 3. Owner / Qualification Requirements

- No statewide owner or qualifier rule was confirmed for a general private security company license

## 4. Business Requirements

- Register the business normally in Kentucky
- Check local city rules, especially if offering armed security

## 5. Insurance / Bond

- No statewide security-company insurance or bond rule confirmed in this pass

## 6. Fees & Validity

- No statewide agency license fee confirmed because no statewide security-company license was identified

## 7. Employee Guard Requirements

- Local armed-security licensing may apply in some cities

## 8. Simple Summary

1. Form the company
2. Check local armed-security rules in the cities where you will operate
3. Carry commercial insurance even if not specifically state-mandated
