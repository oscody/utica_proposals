# Louisiana Security Company License

## 1. License Name

- Company License under the Louisiana State Board of Private Security Examiners
- Regulated by LSBPSE

## 2. Core Requirement

- Louisiana requires a state company license to operate a contract security company.
- The state separately regulates owners, qualifying agents, managers, instructors, and officers.

## 3. Owner / Qualification Requirements

- Company owners / applicants go through LSBPSE application review
- Owners must pass the Louisiana owner exam
- Fingerprints and background review are required

## 4. Business Requirements

- Apply through the LSBPSE portal
- Company licensing is handled directly by the state board

## 5. Insurance / Bond

- Confirm the current liability insurance requirements with the Louisiana company packet before filing

## 6. Fees & Validity

- Total fee for a single company applicant listed by LSBPSE FAQ: $565.72
- Additional applicants can increase the cost

## 7. Employee Guard Requirements

- Officers and other regulated personnel must be registered or licensed through the board

## 8. Simple Summary

1. Form the company
2. Complete the Louisiana company-owner application
3. Submit fingerprints and take the owner exam
4. Pay the board fee through the portal
5. Register or license all officers and related personnel
