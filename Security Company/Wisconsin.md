# Wisconsin Security Company License

## 1. License Name

- Private Detective Agency License
- Regulated by Wisconsin Department of Safety and Professional Services

## 2. Core Requirement

- Wisconsin does not use a separate statewide “security agency” label in the same way many states do.
- Businesses providing guard or patrol services usually fit under the private detective agency licensing structure.

## 3. Owner / Qualification Requirements

- Wisconsin reviews the applicant and agency under state private detective law

## 4. Business Requirements

- Apply through DSPS
- Use the Wisconsin agency application packet

## 5. Insurance / Bond

- Wisconsin requires a $100,000 surety bond for the agency

## 6. Fees & Validity

- Confirm the current application and renewal fee directly with DSPS when filing

## 7. Employee Guard Requirements

- Wisconsin regulates private detective and private security persons through the same licensing framework

## 8. Simple Summary

1. Form the company
2. Use the Wisconsin agency licensing path
3. Post the $100,000 bond
4. Confirm the current fee with DSPS
