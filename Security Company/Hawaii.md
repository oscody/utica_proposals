# Hawaii Security Company License

## 1. License Name

- Guard Agency License
- Regulated by the Hawaii Board of Private Detectives and Guards

## 2. Core Requirement

- Hawaii requires a state guard agency license.
- A guard agency must employ at least one principal guard.

## 3. Owner / Qualification Requirements

- The agency must have a principal guard responsible for direct management and control
- Guard and agency applicants are screened under Hawaii licensing law

## 4. Business Requirements

- Apply through the Hawaii licensing board
- The agency cannot advertise or operate as a guard agency without the license

## 5. Insurance / Bond

- Hawaii requires a surety bond for guard agencies
- Confirm the current bond form and amount with the Hawaii board packet when filing

## 6. Fees & Validity

- Hawaii requires application and license fees for guard agencies
- Confirm the current exact fees with the board at filing

## 7. Employee Guard Requirements

- Individuals working as guards must also be licensed

## 8. Simple Summary

1. Form the company
2. Designate the principal guard
3. Meet Hawaii bond requirements
4. Apply for the guard agency license
5. License each working guard
