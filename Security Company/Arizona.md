# Arizona Security Company License

## 1. License Name

- Security Guard Agency License
- Regulated by the Arizona Department of Public Safety

## 2. Core Requirement

- Arizona requires a state agency license to operate a security guard company.
- The license is issued to the qualifying party.

## 3. Owner / Qualification Requirements

- The company must have a Qualifying Party
- Agency associates, officers, and resident manager registrations may also be required
- Arizona approves agency names before entity filing is finalized

## 4. Business Requirements

- Apply through the DPS SGPI Licensing Unit
- Use an approved business name
- Vehicles and markings must comply with DPS rules

## 5. Insurance / Bond

- Arizona requires the agency filing and agency certification process through DPS
- Confirm current insurance requirements directly in the agency packet when filing

## 6. Fees & Validity

- Agency application: $500
- Agency license certification: $500
- Agency renewal: $500
- Agency restructure: $100
- Qualifying party or resident manager: $50
- Fingerprint fee: $22 per card

## 7. Employee Guard Requirements

- Unarmed guard: $50
- Armed guard: $100
- Guards are licensed separately through DPS

## 8. Simple Summary

1. Get DPS approval for the company name
2. Pick the qualifying party
3. File the agency application and fingerprints
4. Pay the Arizona agency fees
5. License each unarmed or armed guard
