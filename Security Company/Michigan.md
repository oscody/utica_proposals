# Michigan Security Company License

## 1. License Name

- Security Alarm / Guard Agency License
- Regulated by Michigan Department of Licensing and Regulatory Affairs

## 2. Core Requirement

- Michigan requires a state agency license to operate a security guard company.

## 3. Owner / Qualification Requirements

- Applicant must meet Michigan’s agency qualification rules
- Background review and business disclosure are part of the filing

## 4. Business Requirements

- Apply through LARA
- Corporate applicants file through the agency licensing process

## 5. Insurance / Bond

- Michigan requires a $25,000 bond for the agency

## 6. Fees & Validity

- Agency application fee: $300
- Verify current renewal timing and fee directly with LARA when filing

## 7. Employee Guard Requirements

- Michigan regulates agency personnel under the same act
- Armed personnel need to meet the applicable firearms rules

## 8. Simple Summary

1. Form the company
2. Meet Michigan agency qualifications
3. Post the $25,000 bond
4. File through LARA
5. Make sure agency personnel meet Michigan requirements
