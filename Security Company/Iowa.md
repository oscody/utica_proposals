# Iowa Security Company License

## 1. License Name

- Private Security Agency License
- Regulated by the Iowa Department of Public Safety

## 2. Core Requirement

- Iowa requires a state license for private security agencies doing business in Iowa.

## 3. Owner / Qualification Requirements

- Iowa screens agency applicants under Iowa Code Chapter 80A
- Fingerprints and background review are part of the application process

## 4. Business Requirements

- Apply through the Iowa DPS private investigation / private security licensing system
- Iowa provides a dedicated agency application checklist

## 5. Insurance / Bond

- Iowa’s exact agency insurance and bond items should be confirmed from the current agency checklist and Chapter 80A materials

## 6. Fees & Validity

- Iowa uses an online licensing process
- Confirm the current agency fee in the application portal before filing

## 7. Employee Guard Requirements

- Iowa also licenses agency employees working in private security

## 8. Simple Summary

1. Form the company
2. Complete the Iowa agency checklist
3. Submit fingerprints and business materials
4. Confirm the current fee and insurance items in the portal
5. License agency employees separately
