# North Dakota Security Company License

## 1. License Name

- Security Agency License
- Regulated by the North Dakota Private Investigation and Security Board

## 2. Core Requirement

- North Dakota requires a state security-agency license.

## 3. Owner / Qualification Requirements

- North Dakota licenses both providers and agencies
- Agency and provider qualifications are reviewed by the board

## 4. Business Requirements

- Apply through the Private Investigation and Security Board
- Agencies are listed publicly by the board

## 5. Insurance / Bond

- Confirm current agency insurance or bond requirements with the board before filing

## 6. Fees & Validity

- North Dakota uses board-set fees
- Confirm the current exact agency fee with the board

## 7. Employee Guard Requirements

- Security providers are licensed separately

## 8. Simple Summary

1. Form the company
2. Apply for the North Dakota agency license
3. Confirm current fee and insurance items with the board
4. License the individual providers separately
