# Colorado Security Company License

## 1. License Name

- No statewide private security company license currently identified in this directory

## 2. Core Requirement

- Colorado has not historically required a statewide security company license.
- Local city or county rules may still apply.

## 3. Owner / Qualification Requirements

- No current statewide qualifier requirement found for a general private security company license
- Local licensing may impose local standards

## 4. Business Requirements

- Register the business normally with Colorado
- Check the city or county where guards will actually work

## 5. Insurance / Bond

- No statewide security-company insurance rule confirmed in this pass
- Contracts and local rules may still require insurance

## 6. Fees & Validity

- No statewide company license fee currently applies because there is no general statewide license in place yet

## 7. Employee Guard Requirements

- Guard licensing is generally local rather than statewide in Colorado

## 8. Simple Summary

1. Form the company
2. Check the city and county rules where you want to operate
3. Handle local licensing if required
4. Carry commercial insurance even if not state-mandated
