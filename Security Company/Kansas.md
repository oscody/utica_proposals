# Kansas Security Company License

## 1. License Name

- No statewide private security company license identified in this directory

## 2. Core Requirement

- Kansas is treated here as a no-statewide-license state for general private security company operations.
- Kansas does license private detective agencies, which is a different track.

## 3. Owner / Qualification Requirements

- No statewide owner or qualifier rule was confirmed for a general security guard company
- Local city rules may apply

## 4. Business Requirements

- Register the business normally in Kansas
- Check the city or county where the guards will work

## 5. Insurance / Bond

- No statewide security-company insurance or bond rule confirmed in this pass

## 6. Fees & Validity

- No statewide agency license fee is listed here because no statewide security-company license was identified

## 7. Employee Guard Requirements

- Security licensing appears to be local rather than statewide

## 8. Simple Summary

1. Form the company
2. Check city and county requirements
3. Do not confuse Kansas private detective licensing with general security-company licensing
