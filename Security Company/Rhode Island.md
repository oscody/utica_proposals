# Rhode Island Security Company License

## 1. License Name

- Security Guard Business License
- Regulated by the Rhode Island Office of the Attorney General, Bureau of Criminal Identification

## 2. Core Requirement

- If you want to operate a private security guard business in Rhode Island, you need this business license.
- Rhode Island is fairly direct, but it requires a Rhode Island address or registered agent and full owner background screening.

## 3. Owner / Qualification Requirements

- Every applicant, partner, officer, general manager, and each shareholder owning 10% or more must meet the license qualifications
- National background check required for those individuals on new and renewal applications
- Felony convictions are disqualifying

## 4. Business Requirements

- Submit the notarized Rhode Island security business application
- Must have:
  - a physical Rhode Island address properly zoned for business, or
  - a Rhode Island registered agent
- No P.O. Box for the business address
- Submit Rhode Island certificate of authority or letter of good standing from the Secretary of State

## 5. Insurance / Bond

- Proof of insurance required:
  - $300,000 for bodily or personal injury
  - $100,000 for property damage
- The application packet also asks whether the business has the required surety bond under Rhode Island law

## 6. Fees & Validity

- Biennial application / renewal fee: $400
- Duplicate out-of-state copy fee if requested: $100
- Individual owner background check fee: $45 each
- License term: 2 years

## 7. Employee Guard Requirements

- All employees working under the license must undergo a national background check before starting employment
- The company cannot knowingly employ a person convicted of a felony
- Armed-security operations also require compliance with Rhode Island firearms laws

## 8. Simple Summary

1. Form the company and qualify it in Rhode Island
2. Set up a Rhode Island address or registered agent
3. Obtain the required insurance
4. Submit the notarized business license packet with owner background checks
5. Background-check every employee before they start work
