# Delaware Security Company License

## 1. License Name

- Class B Private Security Agency License
- Regulated by the Delaware State Police Professional Licensing Section and the Board of Examiners of Private Security

## 2. Core Requirement

- If you want to operate a private security agency in Delaware, you need a Class B agency license.
- Delaware also requires the guards themselves to be separately licensed.

## 3. Owner / Qualification Requirements

- Applicant must be at least 25 years old
- Must have at least:
  - 4 years of experience as a manager in a licensed security agency, or
  - 5 years of investigative experience, or
  - prior law-enforcement service with academy graduation
- No felony conviction
- No disqualifying misdemeanor involving theft, drugs, or moral turpitude within the last 7 years, subject to the statute’s limits

## 4. Business Requirements

- Apply through Delaware State Police Professional Licensing
- If the agency has no office in Delaware, it must report a compliance agent for the business
- The compliance agent must maintain all agency records within Delaware and make them available to the Section on demand

## 5. Insurance / Bond

- Surety bond required
- Certificate of liability insurance required
- Delaware law leaves the amount to the Board

## 6. Fees & Validity

- Delaware law allows the Board to charge an agency application fee up to $600 per year
- Confirm the current exact Class B agency fee with Professional Licensing before filing

## 7. Employee Guard Requirements

- Unarmed guards need the Delaware security guard license (“Yellow Card”)
- Armed guards need the armed endorsement (“Red Card”)
- Unarmed guards must complete mandatory training and pass the state exam
- Unarmed guard licenses are valid for 5 years

## 8. Simple Summary

1. Form the company
2. Make sure the agency owner meets Delaware’s experience rule
3. Get the required bond and liability insurance
4. File for the Class B agency license with DSP Professional Licensing
5. Hire only properly licensed Delaware guards
