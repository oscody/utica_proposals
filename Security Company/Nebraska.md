# Nebraska Security Company License

## 1. License Name

- No separate statewide security company license clearly identified

## 2. Core Requirement

- Nebraska does not appear to have a separate statewide contract security company license in the same structure used by many other states.
- If a service crosses into private detective work, different licensing rules may apply.

## 3. Owner / Qualification Requirements

- No statewide owner or qualifier rule was confirmed for a general security-company license

## 4. Business Requirements

- Register the business normally in Nebraska
- Check local rules where the guards will work
- Review whether the planned service triggers Nebraska private detective rules

## 5. Insurance / Bond

- No statewide security-company insurance or bond rule confirmed in this pass

## 6. Fees & Validity

- No statewide security-company agency fee confirmed because no general statewide license was identified

## 7. Employee Guard Requirements

- Local or assignment-specific requirements may apply

## 8. Simple Summary

1. Form the company
2. Check local licensing rules
3. Check whether any planned services fall under private detective regulation
