# Submission Requirements

Source: Sections `2.4 Key Events/Timeline`, `2.5 Submission of Proposal`, and nearby instruction text from the WMCHealth RFP markdown.

## Proposal Due Date

Proposals must be emailed no later than `4:00 PM Eastern Standard Time on April 20, 2026`.

## Required Email Subject

`WFA0FE5 Security Guard Services RFP Proposal`

## Delivery Method

The proposal must be emailed to the designated contacts identified in the RFP.

## Late Proposals

Proposals received after the deadline will not be considered. WMCHealth states that it is not responsible for internal or external delivery delays.

## Key Timeline

| Event | Date |
| --- | --- |
| RFP Release | March 30, 2026 |
| Letter of Intent | April 3, 2026 |
| Questions and Requests for Clarification Due | April 8, 2026 |
| Questions and Answers Distributed | April 14, 2026 |
| Proposal Due Date | April 20, 2026 |
| Tentative Contract Start Date | July 1, 2026 |

## Cost Rule

Proposers are responsible for all costs of preparing and submitting the proposal and attending any conference or interview. WMCHealth will not reimburse those costs.

## Addenda Rule

If WMCHealth revises any part of the RFP or extends a deadline, it will issue an addendum to all known prospective proposers.
