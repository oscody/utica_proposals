Source: Sections `2.3`, `2.5`, and `2.6` from the WMCHealth RFP markdown.

## Restricted Period Rule

From the date the RFP is issued until tentative award and approval of any contract, all communications between vendors and WMCHealth regarding the RFP must go only through the designated contacts, unless an exception applies.

## Permissible Contacts

The RFP lists these permissible contacts:

- submission of written proposals
- written complaints to the General Counsel regarding failure to timely respond to authorized contacts
- participation in RFP conferences or interviews
- negotiations following tentative award
- requests to review the award of a contract
- legal or administrative proceedings regarding the award of a contract

## Record of Contacts

WMCHealth will record information about vendor contacts during the restricted period as part of the procurement record.

## Non-Responsibility Disclosure and Affirmation

The proposal must include:

- Attachment D disclosure of prior non-responsibility determinations
- Attachment E written affirmation agreeing to comply with permissible-contact procedures

The RFP states that proposals without a signed original affirmation may be rejected.
