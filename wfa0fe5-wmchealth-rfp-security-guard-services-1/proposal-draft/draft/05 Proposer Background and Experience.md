# Proposer Background and Experience

## 1.1 Firm Background

Utica Security Services Inc. is a licensed, bonded, and fully insured New York security services firm providing professional guard services in public-facing, high-activity, and operationally sensitive environments. The company specializes in reliable, compliant, and customer-focused security solutions for facilities that require continuous coverage, strong supervision, and visible professionalism. Its operating approach is designed for contracts where coverage integrity, rapid escalation, and clear accountability are essential.

Founded on the principles of discipline, professionalism, and integrity, Utica Security Services Inc. has developed a service model built around structured field supervision, management responsiveness, workforce compliance, and clear reporting expectations. The firm places strong emphasis on regulatory compliance, workforce training, accountability, and proactive communication with clients. This model is well suited for healthcare settings where security personnel must balance public interaction, patient-facing sensitivity, emergency readiness, and operational discipline.

## 1.2 Range of Services

Utica Security Services Inc. provides and manages services that include:

- Fixed-post security coverage
- Roving patrols and perimeter checks
- Access control and visitor management
- Incident response and escalation support
- De-escalation-focused public-facing security services
- Control desk and monitoring functions
- Supervisory oversight and quality assurance inspections
- Staffing management, relief coverage, and 24/7 on-call escalation
- Incident documentation and electronic reporting support

Based on the existing RGRTA proposal materials, the firm's core service strengths can also be described as:

- Site supervision and 24/7 on-call management
