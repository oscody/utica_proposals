# Fee Proposal

The WMCHealth RFP requires the fee proposal to be submitted in a separate, clearly marked document.

This file is a placeholder for the pricing package and should be replaced with a final fee proposal that includes:

- Clear pricing cover sheet
- Site-by-site or task-by-task pricing assumptions
- Labor categories and hourly rates as applicable
- Armed and unarmed rate structure if applicable
- Supervisory and management pricing assumptions
- Vehicle, equipment, and uniform cost treatment
- Transition or startup cost assumptions, if any
- Subtotals by task and phase of work
- Explicit assumptions, exclusions, and validity period

No pricing was inferred or generated in this draft because the workspace did not contain validated staffing quantities, current wage assumptions, or company-approved rate sheets.
