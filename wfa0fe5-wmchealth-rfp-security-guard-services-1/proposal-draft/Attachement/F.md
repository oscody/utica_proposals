Q1 — Are you a minority-owned business?  
 A minority business requires 51% or more owned by: Blacks, Hispanics, Asians, American  
 Indians, Eskimos, or Aleuts.

You need to answer this — only the company owner can confirm the ownership percentage.

Q2 — Are you a women-owned business?  
 Requires women to own at least 51% of the firm.

If No to both Q1 and Q2:

- Q3 and Q4 are skipped
- Sign and date — form is done  


If Yes to either Q1 or Q2:

- Q3: State the ownership percentage
- Q4: List the names of minority/women owners and each person's ownership percentage
- Sign and date
